Public Running Days:

First sunday of every month

Time:

From 9:00am to 12:00pm

Where:

You can find us in Leichardt Park on the corner of Campbelle and Cambridge Street in Rockhampton

Cost:

$2 for three laps or $10 for unlimited rides

Note:

Fully enclosed footwear must be worn to ride the trains

Who are we

We are a small club that meets on the first Sunday of every month providing the weather is good, We run mini trains for the public to ride. We currently have 10 steam trains, 4 Desiel and 3 Electric apart of our club. Most of these Train can be regulary seen at our monthly runday. Our club currendly consests of about 20 members with more joining us occasionally.
