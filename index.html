Public Running Days:

The first Sunday of every month

Time:

From: 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM

Where:

You can find us in Leichhardt Park on the corner of Campbell and Cambridge Street in Rockhampton

Cost:

$2 for three laps or $10 for unlimited rides

Note:

Fully enclosed footwear must be worn to ride the trains

Who are we

We are a small club that meets on the first Sunday of every month providing the weather is fit for running. We operate mini trains for the public to ride. We currently have about 10 steam trains, 4 diesels and 3 electrics apart of our club, most of these trains can be seen regulary at our public running days on the first Sunday of every month from 9:00am till 12:00pm. Our club currently consists of about 25 members with more joining occasionally.

Private Functions

We do also offer a private function option, if you would like a private booking please make a booking request through our form on the bookings page. More information can also be found there.
